Nice to meet you!

Welcome to v1.3 of my website! Hopefully more to come as I continue to explore more about design & code. ☺

I'm Sabrina Paramitha—an Interaction Design student at Sheridan College. In my spare time, I enjoy drawing, gaming, and learning new skills. I believe that anything can be taught as long as you have patience with yourself.

Experiences are remembered. I always put the user at the core of my designs which is why I have a passion for user research. By working with users and taking the time to understand their needs, a good experience can be made.

Currently learning Godot, 3D modelling, and motion design in my spare time. (I made the animation on the homepage!)

MY VALUES

Growth

I'm open to challenges and opportunities to better my work, as well as help others thrive!

Accessibility

I believe that accessibility is for everyone and practice usability standards in my work.

Transparency

I wish to be a part of and contribute to an environment where everyone can comfortably express their thoughts and feelings.

Enjoyment

I strive for a positive mindset to experience joy in everyday things and hope my work can bring users little joys through interactions.
